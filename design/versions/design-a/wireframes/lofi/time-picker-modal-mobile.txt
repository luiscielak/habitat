Annotations:
A1: Modal presented as sheet from bottom of screen
A2: Native iOS time picker (wheel-style scrolling)
A3: User scrolls to select hour, minute, AM/PM
A4: "Done" button saves time and dismisses sheet
A5: Tapping outside sheet or swiping down dismisses without saving
A6: Selected time persists for future days (until changed)
A7: Time format: 12-hour with AM/PM (or device setting)
A8: Sheet has glass effect background (per design system)

Flow Hooks:
← Daily View (dismiss sheet, return to Daily View)
↑ Tap "Done" → Save time, update habit, dismiss sheet
↑ Swipe down → Dismiss without saving
