Annotations:
A1: Selected action card has visual highlight (different background)
A2: Inline form appears immediately below selected action
A3: Form fields vary by action type (workout, meal, hungry, etc.)
A4: Primary action button at bottom of form (e.g., "Add activity")
A5: Form can be dismissed by tapping action card again
A6: After form submission, insight card appears below form
A7: Users can scroll up to select different action
A8: Loading state: Progress indicator while generating response
A9: Empty state: No form shown until action is selected

Flow Hooks:
→ Daily View (via tab bar)
→ Weekly View (via tab bar)
↑ Tap different action → Switch to that action's form
↑ Submit form → Show insight card with coaching response
↑ Tap action again → Dismiss form
